Who we are

What we do

We specialize in healthcare IT systems consulting, with particular expertise in SystmOne implementations. Our goal is to maximize resource utilization, improve patient care, and establish user groups to share system experiences.

SystmOne Implementation

Expert implementation and customization of SystmOne systems for hospices, care homes, and healthcare organizations across the UK.

Healthcare Systems Design

Custom systems design and integration to meet the real-world requirements of healthcare providers and improve team collaboration.

Training & Support

Comprehensive training programs and ongoing support to ensure teams can maximize the benefits of their healthcare IT systems.

User Groups & Community

Establishing and supporting user groups including the Sussex-wide SystmOne User Group and Isle of Wight SystmOne User Group.

Who we've helped

Over our 20+ year history, we've had the privilege of working with numerous healthcare organizations, helping them transform their IT systems and improve patient care.
